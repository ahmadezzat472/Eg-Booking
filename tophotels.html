Frequently asked questions about hotels on Booking.com

How do I find budget hotels on eg-booking.com ?

There are several ways to find budget hotels on Booking.com. You can filter hotels by price to show only those that match your budget, or you can sort by price to show the hotels at the lowest price first. If you are a member of our Genius loyalty program, you can also enjoy discounted rates at select hotels and other accommodations.

Where can I find hotel deals on eg-booking.com ?

We have a range of different offers on hotels throughout the year, and you can find them all on our Deals page. If you are a member of our Genius loyalty program, you can also log into your account to see discounted rates at select hotels and other accommodations..

How many hotels are listed on eg-Booking.com ?

TThere are currently 21,622,341 hotels listed on eg-Booking.com, so you'll always be able to find the perfect hotel wherever you're headed!

How do I search for a hotel on eg-Booking.com ?

To search for a hotel on Booking.com, all you need is your destination, travel dates, and the number of people traveling in your group. Don't know your destination yet or when you want to travel? You can then browse our site for inspiration and find your ideal hotel that way.

What makes eg-Booking.com hotel reviews reliable?

You can trust hotel reviews on eg-Booking.com because guests can leave a review only after they've stayed at the hotel. This means that you will see verified reviews written by real guests on eg-Booking.com.
